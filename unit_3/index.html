Joe's was originally created and operated as a "Mobile Guerilla" Coffee Cart on the Santa Monica beaches in 1972.

After 10 years, and many a trip to the Caribbean, Joe and his traveling buddy (Levan), finally set up a brick and mortar cafe in 1985.
